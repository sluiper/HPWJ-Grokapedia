# Appendix E: Training & Competency Quick Matrix

**Status:** Draft on `draft/appendices` – synthesis of Chapter 13 + MCR-030, MCR-042, MCR-049, MCR-053, MCR-064  
**Purpose:** One-page view of who needs what training before they can touch the equipment.

---

## E.1 Core Requirements (All Pressure Classes)

| Role | Minimum Requirements | MCR Link |
|------|----------------------|----------|
| Any HPWJ operator | Method-specific certification (Anabeeb Training Dept / TQCP or equivalent) + experience | MCR-042, MCR-030 |
| Manual lance / gun operator | Above + **minimum 2 years continuous relevant experience** + Management approval | MCR-049 |
| Supervisor / Authorising Person | Above + supervisor module + stop-work authority training | MCR-036, MCR-041 |
| Marine / Diver | Above + IMCA D049 (or equivalent) diver jetting competence | MCR-053 |
| UHP Creeping Hose | Level 3 operators only + AWD verified | MCR-034 |

---

## E.2 Refresh & Integration of Lessons

- All personnel trained and assessed against the Master Control Register (MCR-030)
- New lessons that map to RPN > 150 or new failure modes must be incorporated into the next ATC training cycle (MCR-064)
- Case 2 (documented UK HSE flexi-lance fatality) is mandatory on Day 1 and Day 3 of every course (Section 25 + Section 27)

---

## E.3 Quick Check Before Mobilisation

- [ ] Method-specific card current
- [ ] Experience recorded and Management approval obtained if manual work
- [ ] Medical Alert Card issued and carried
- [ ] Supervisor has verified competency records
- [ ] Latest Lessons Learned (Section 27) briefed if any new high-RPN items exist
