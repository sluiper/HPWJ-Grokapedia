## B.4 Selection Heuristics (from existing synthesis)

- General industrial / oil & gas mobile work → NLB-style diesel triplex + appropriate tooling.
- Multi-operator or ATEX environments → WOMA features become relevant.
- High automation / reduced operator exposure on tube bundles → Peinemann or Hammelmann robotic systems + competent Level 3 operators (MCR-034 / 021).
- Rotating / flex tooling quality and reaction-force geometry → StoneAge paired with a proven pump OEM.
- Always apply the existing MCR controls (reaction force, AWD, pre-use, dump, competency) regardless of brand.
