## ABSOLUTE NEVER RULES (Anabeeb HPWJ)

### Equipment & Setup
- **NEVER** start work without a completed, documented, and supervisor-verified pre-use inspection (MCR-023 / RPN 210).
- **NEVER** use a flexible lance without a correctly installed and verified Anti-Withdrawal / Anti-Ejection Device (AWD) (MCR-019 / MCR-020 / RPN 200).
- **NEVER** leave the pump running unattended (MCR-011).
- **NEVER** reuse a rupture disc. Always have a spare of the correct rating on the unit (MCR-009 / MCR-048).
- **NEVER** exceed 40 000 psi (2 759 bar) — absolute Anabeeb ceiling (MCR-046).
- **NEVER** use quick-connects on high-pressure hoses where relative movement or rotation is possible (MCR-007 / RPN 180).
- **NEVER** operate with a reaction force > 250 N absolute or > 1/3 body weight or with geometry that allows the jet to cross the operator’s feet/legs (MCR-016 / RPN 180).

### People & Competency
- **NEVER** allow lone working on pressurised HPWJ equipment (MCR-040 / MCR-047).
- **NEVER** permit manual lance/gun work without minimum 2 years continuous relevant experience + Management approval (MCR-049).
- **NEVER** allow an unauthorised person within 10 metres of operating equipment (MCR-051).
- **NEVER** bypass stop-work authority. Any person can stop the job (MCR-036 / MCR-038).

### Operations
- **NEVER** perform HPWJ adjacent to hot work or other incompatible activities without daily SIMOPS meeting, named coordinator, and physical zoning (MCR-028 / RPN 200).
- **NEVER** continue work if communication (radio or line-of-sight) is lost (MCR-025).
- **NEVER** ignore weather: stop for lightning; apply heat-stress controls in KSA conditions (MCR-032 / MCR-033).
- **NEVER** use worn, pitted, or life-expired nozzles or hoses (MCR-001 / MCR-004 / MCR-018).

### Marine / Special
- **NEVER** allow a diver to operate HPWJ without IMCA D049 (or equivalent) competence and full surface support (MCR-053).
- **NEVER** allow free-floating high-pressure hoses underwater (MCR-055).
