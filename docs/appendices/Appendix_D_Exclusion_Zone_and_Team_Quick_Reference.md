# Appendix D: Exclusion Zone & Minimum Team Quick Reference

**Status:** Live on main (merged after clean verification)  
**Source:** MCR-027, MCR-031, MCR-047, MCR-051, MCR-057 + Chapter 5  
**Purpose:** One-page field card for supervisors setting up the work area.

---

## D.1 Exclusion Zone Sizing (MCR-031 + MCR-027 + MCR-051)

| Pressure Class | Minimum Exclusion (typical) | Anabeeb Unauthorised Persons | Notes |
|----------------|-----------------------------|------------------------------|-------|
| ≤ 10 000 psi   | 7.5 – 10 m                  | **10 m**                     | Adjust for jet direction and possible whip |
| 10 – 20 kpsi   | 10 – 15 m                   | **10 m**                     | |
| UHP / 40 kpsi  | 15 – 25 m+                  | **10 m**                     | Expanded for creeping hose / flex lance |
| Marine / Dock  | Line-of-fire + barriers     | No non-essential in immediate area (MCR-057) | Dock bottom / moon-pool treated as high-risk |

**Absolute Anabeeb rule (MCR-051):** No unauthorised person within **10 metres**. System shall be depressurised on breach.

SABIC baseline is 6 m; Anabeeb is stricter at 10 m for unauthorised persons.

## D.2 Minimum Team Size

| Context | Minimum Competent Persons | Source |
|---------|---------------------------|--------|
| Land-based Anabeeb HPWJ | **3** (one dedicated to emergency dump when LOS lost) | MCR-047 |
| SABIC baseline | 2 | MCR-040 |
| Marine / Diving | Surface tender + standby diver + diving supervisor + HPWJ supervisor (higher manning) | MCR-053 + Section 23 |
| Lone working | **Prohibited** | MCR-040 / MCR-047 |

---

## D.3 Key Non-Negotiables for Setup

- Physical barricading + pressure-appropriate exclusion zone (MCR-027)
- Clear line-of-sight or dedicated emergency-stop person (MCR-025)
- Pre-use inspection completed and supervisor-verified before permit (MCR-023 + MCR-024)
- Whip checks both sides of every HP connection (MCR-006)
- Medical Alert Cards carried by all operators (MCR-043 / MCR-058)
- SIMOPS interfaces identified and coordinated if present (MCR-028)

---

## D.4 Quick Barricading Checklist

- [ ] Hard barriers or robust tape + signs at the calculated distance
- [ ] Entry/exit control point established
- [ ] “Danger – High Pressure Water Jetting” signage visible from all approaches
- [ ] Wind direction considered (mist drift)
- [ ] Adjacent activities (hot work, traffic, other contractors) assessed for SIMOPS
