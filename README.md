## Purpose
This is the single most complete, maximum-depth reference for High Pressure Water Jetting (HPWJ / Hydroblasting) operations, safety, equipment, training, and global best practices.

It combines:
- Operational procedures and checklists
- Maximum technical depth (pump hydraulics, flow vs pressure, physics)
- Every identified control and numeric threshold
- Global manufacturer variations (NLB, StoneAge, Hammelmann, WOMA, Peinemann, Kamat, etc.)
- Clinical and forensic lessons from worldwide incident data
- Training & competency frameworks
- Regulatory comparisons
- Environmental and human factors considerations
