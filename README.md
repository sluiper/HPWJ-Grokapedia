**Critical Correction Applied:** Section 16.4 jet velocity worked examples were previously understated by a factor of ≈√10 due to a unit conversion error. Values have been recalculated from first principles (now ~371 m/s at 10k psi, ~525 m/s at 20k psi, ~743 m/s at 40k psi). Reaction-force worked example also revised for consistency. This is the kind of error that a proper independent check is meant to catch.
