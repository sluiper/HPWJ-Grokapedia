# Whip Check & Hose Inspection Card (Field Pocket Card)

**Anabeeb HPWJ – Controlled Template**  
**Print double-sided, laminate, keep with every high-pressure hose set**

---

### FRONT – Whip Check Inspection (MCR-006)

**Before every connection:**

☐ Whip check present on **both sides** of the connection  
☐ Correct Working Load Limit (WLL) for this hose size + pressure  
☐ No fraying, corrosion, broken wires, or deformation  
☐ Correctly attached and free to restrain a hose failure  
☐ Not used as the primary connection method

**Fail any item → DO NOT USE. Replace immediately.**

**Preferred**: Manufacturer-recommended or HPWJ-rated whip checks (e.g. DERC Salotech / Red Dog rated models, NLB, StoneAge guidance).

---

### BACK – Hose Quick Inspection (MCR-001 to MCR-005)

**Reject immediately if any of these are present:**

☐ Exposed steel wire braid / reinforcement  
☐ Bulging, blistering, or bubbling of outer cover  
☐ Cuts or abrasion reaching the reinforcement  
☐ Kinks, permanent deformation, or exceeded bend radius  
☐ Weepage or leakage  
☐ Damaged, corroded, or loose end fittings  
☐ Exceeded service life (Flex/whip = 2 years | Standard = 4 years)  
☐ Missing or illegible MAWP / colour coding / expiration marking  
☐ No current annual third-party pressure test record

**Colour coding must be visible from 2 feet and matched to system pressure.**
