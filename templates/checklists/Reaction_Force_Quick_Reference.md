# Reaction Force Quick Reference Card (MCR-016)

**Anabeeb HPWJ – Pocket Card**  
**Three Additive Controls – ALL must be satisfied for handheld work**

---

### The Three Additive Controls

1. **Absolute limit** ≤ **250 N**
2. **Body weight ratio** ≤ **1/3 of operator body weight**
3. **Barrel geometry** – operator must not be able to pass the jet over their own feet or legs

**If any one fails → do not use handheld. Switch to mechanical assistance, longer barrel, different nozzle, or automation.**

---

### Practical Formulas (for planning)

**Imperial**:  
Reaction Force (lbf) ≈ 0.052 × Q (GPM) × √P (psi)

**Metric**:  
Reaction Force (N) ≈ 0.745 × Q (L/min) × √P (bar)

**Example**: 12 GPM at 15,000 psi ≈ 76 lbf ≈ 340 N → **exceeds 250 N limit** → not safe for handheld without additional controls.

---

### Field Decision

☐ Calculated / assessed force ≤ 250 N  
☐ ≤ 1/3 of the specific operator’s body weight  
☐ Barrel long enough / stance correct so jet cannot cross own body  
☐ Operator trained and fit for the force level

**All boxes ticked?** → Proceed with continuous monitoring.  
**Any box unticked?** → Stop and redesign the tooling / method.
