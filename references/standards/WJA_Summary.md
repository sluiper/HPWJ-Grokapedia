# WJA (Water Jetting Association – UK) – Standards & Training Deep Dive

**Source**: WJA official training scheme, Codes of Practice, and accredited providers (2025–2026)

## Codes of Practice
- **Blue Code**: High Pressure and Ultra-High Pressure water jetting (core for industrial HPWJ)
- **Red Code**: Drain and sewer jetting
- **Black Code** (unified 2025/2026): Single comprehensive code covering all water jetting types from pressure washing to UHP. Underpins all training. Available via WJA App.

Codes are ISBN-designated, regularly updated with HSE input, and internationally recognised.

## Training Pathway (City & Guilds / ABBE accredited)

### 1. Water Jetting Safety Awareness (Mandatory Entry)
- 1-day classroom theory
- Covers: types of water jetting, pressure/flow, hazards (especially injection injuries), risk assessment, PPE, emergency response, Code of Practice
- Every operative must complete this before practical work
- Certificate + photo card elements

### 2. Practical Modules
- Surface Preparation, Drain Cleaning, Tank/Vessel, etc.
- Hands-on under approved instructors

### 3. Level 2 Water Jetting Technician Certificate
- Safety Awareness + ≥2 practical modules + 122 hours guided workplace assessment supervised by WJA Assessor
- Outcome: Recognised as competent operative, digital operational jetting card, entry into WJA database

### Refresher
- Competent Safety Awareness refresher every 3 years
