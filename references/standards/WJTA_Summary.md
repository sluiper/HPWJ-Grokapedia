# WJTA (WaterJet Technology Association) – Standards & Training Deep Dive

**Source**: Official WJTA resources (2026)

## Core Document
**Industry Best Practices for the Use of High Pressure Waterjetting Equipment** ("Orange Book" – Third Edition). Pocket Mini Orange Book also available for field use.

## Hydroblaster Training and Certification Program

### Foundational Training (FT – 19HBFT)
- 3-hour eLearning module
- Covers recommended safety practices aligned with Best Practices
- 80% pass on multiple-choice quiz
- Valid for 3 years
- Deliverable via HASC / ARSC / online proctoring
- Price: ~$35–40

### Field Verification (FV – 19HBFV)
- Hands-on skills assessment by WJTA Verified Trainer
- Typically 1.5 days (varies with experience)
- Covers: system setup, pre-job inspection, shotgunning, flex lancing, line moleing, manual & automated methods, job completion
- Must hold valid FT first
- No expiration once achieved
- Credentials transferable and stored in HASC LINK database

### Verified Trainer (VT) & Verified Training Site (VTS)
- Rigorous train-the-trainer + audit by Master Trainer
- Only approved sites and trainers can deliver FV
- Ensures consistency and quality

### Master Trainer
- Oversees program integrity, audits, and continuous development

## Key Strengths for Anabeeb
- Extremely practical and equipment-focused
- Strong pre-use inspection and multi-application coverage (shotgun, flex lance, mole)
- Transferable credentials reduce re-training when personnel move between contractors
- Excellent model for ATC practical assessment checklists

Adopt the FT + FV structure as the practical backbone of Anabeeb Level 2 Operator competency. Map FV checklist items directly to MCR controls.
