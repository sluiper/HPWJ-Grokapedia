# KSA / Anabeeb ATC Context – Standards Integration Notes

**Purpose**: Bridge global best practice to local requirements for the Anabeeb Training Center (ATC) in Majd Square / Jubail area.

## Relevant Local Bodies
- **TVTC** (Technical and Vocational Training Corporation): Accreditation and site approval process for ATC. Courses should be designed for potential TVTC recognition / licensing.
- **ECITB**: Existing relationship via MJI-10 and Membership Services Portal. Explore any future HPWJ or industrial cleaning modules. Use ECITB quality systems as a model for ATC delivery.
- **Client Standards**: Aramco, SABIC, PETRO RABIGH typically require company-approved training + demonstration of competency. The hybrid global model (WJTA practical + WJA theory + 2-year VOC + Medical Alert) is designed to exceed typical client expectations.
- **NEBOSH**: General HSE principles (risk assessment, hierarchy of controls, human factors, emergency procedures) should be woven into all ATC courses for broader QHSSE alignment.

## Practical Implementation Advice for ATC
1. Base the 3-day flagship course on the hybrid model described in Chapter 13.
2. Issue Anabeeb-branded certificates + the Medical Alert Card from templates/medical_alert/.
3. Maintain a digital competency register (name, level, expiry, MCR assessment results).
4. Conduct internal trainer audits and continuous curriculum updates linked to FMEA and incidents.
5. Market specialty modules (UHP tube bundle, automated systems) as high-value revenue products once core competency is established.

This positions Anabeeb ATC as the regional centre of excellence for HPWJ competency in the Eastern Province.
