# Interpump Group / Hawk (Italy) - Manufacturer Reference

**Category**: High-Pressure Pump OEM

## Overview
Interpump Group (including the Hawk brand) is one of the largest global manufacturers of high-pressure pumps. They produce a wide range of plunger pumps used across industrial cleaning, agriculture, and other sectors.

## Key Products
- High-pressure plunger pumps (various pressure and flow ratings)
- Complete pump units for industrial applications
- Components and accessories for high-pressure systems

## Notable Safety & Design Features
- Broad product range covering many pressure and flow combinations.
- Reliable Italian engineering with good global availability.
- Suitable for both mobile and stationary applications.

## Specific Guidance Relevant to Anabeeb
- Solid, cost-effective option for general high-pressure water jetting work.
- Less specialized in ultra-high pressure or advanced automation compared to some German brands (Kamat, Hammelmann, WOMA).
- Good alternative when seeking European pump technology with competitive pricing.

## Strengths
- Wide product range and availability.
- Good balance of performance and cost.
- Strong global manufacturing and distribution network.

## Limitations / Notes
- Less prominent in the most demanding oil & gas or ultra-high pressure segments compared to NLB, WOMA, or Kamat.
- Not as frequently referenced in specialized HPWJ safety literature as some competitors.

## Relevant Never Rules
- Follow standard high-pressure water jetting safety practices (no unique rules beyond general best practice).
