# Kamat (Germany) - Manufacturer Reference

**Category**: High-Pressure Pump OEM (Ultra-High Pressure Specialist)

## Overview
Kamat is a German manufacturer specializing in ultra-high pressure water jetting pumps and systems. They are known for pushing the boundaries of pressure capability.

## Key Products
- Ultra-high pressure pumps (often 50,000+ psi capability)
- Complete high-pressure water jetting units
- Specialized systems for demanding applications

## Notable Safety & Design Features
- Focus on very high-pressure applications where standard pumps are insufficient.
- Robust German engineering.
- Good hazardous area options.

## Specific Guidance Relevant to Anabeeb
- Suitable for specialized applications requiring very high pressure (e.g., certain coating removal or cutting tasks).
- Higher pressure capability comes with increased safety requirements and component stress.
- Not the most common choice for general tube bundle or surface cleaning work (where 10k–20k psi is usually sufficient).

## Strengths
- Very high pressure capability.
- Quality German manufacturing.
- Good for niche ultra-high pressure applications.

## Limitations / Notes
- Higher pressure = higher component wear and stricter safety requirements.
- Over-specified for many standard Anabeeb applications.
- Best used when the job genuinely requires ultra-high pressure.

- Never operate ultra-high pressure systems without appropriate training, PPE, and safety controls.
