# Hammelmann (Germany) - Manufacturer Reference

**Category**: Pump + Tooling & Robotic Systems

## Overview
Hammelmann is a premium German manufacturer of high-pressure pumps and advanced water jetting systems, with a strong focus on safety, automation, and robotic applications.

## Key Products
- High-pressure triplex pumps
- JETMASTER robotic water jetting systems
- xPRO sensor-based safety modules
- PIPEMASTER rotary hose reel feeder systems
- Advanced tooling and accessories

## Notable Safety & Design Features
- **xPRO safety module**: Sensor in the gun detects irregular movements and automatically shuts off high-pressure water.
- Strong emphasis on robotic and remote operation safety.
- Good integration between pumps and advanced tooling.

## Specific Guidance Relevant to Anabeeb
- Robotic and sensor-based systems require specific training and competency.
- PIPEMASTER-style rotary hose reel systems have distinct entanglement and reel-tension hazards compared to standard setups.
- Excellent option when high levels of automation or safety interlocks are required.

## Strengths
- Advanced safety technology (xPRO module)
- Strong robotic/automated systems
- High-quality German engineering

## Limitations / Notes
- Higher cost compared to basic triplex units.
- Best suited for applications where automation and advanced safety features deliver clear value.

## Relevant Never Rules
- Never bypass or disable sensor-based safety modules.
