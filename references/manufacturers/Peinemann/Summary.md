# Peinemann Equipment (Netherlands) - Manufacturer Reference

**Category**: Tooling & Automation Specialist (Automated Tube Bundle Cleaning)

## Overview
Peinemann is a specialist Dutch company focused on hands-free and semi-automated high-pressure water jetting systems, particularly for tube bundle cleaning in heat exchangers. They are widely respected in the oil & gas and petrochemical industries for improving safety and productivity in confined space and high-volume cleaning work.

## Key Products
- Hands-free and semi-automated tube bundle cleaning systems (up to 40,000 psi)
- Flexible lance and automated feed systems
- Associated safety and control equipment

## Notable Safety & Design Features
- Strong focus on removing operators from direct exposure to high-pressure jets and reaction forces.
- Designed specifically for high-volume, repetitive tube cleaning during turnarounds.
- Emphasis on controlled feed rates and entanglement prevention.

## Specific Guidance Relevant to Anabeeb
- Excellent option for large-scale heat exchanger cleaning during major turnarounds.
- Requires specific training and competency for automated/semi-automated operation.
- Different hazard profile compared to traditional manual lance work (entanglement, feed control, and remote operation safety are key).
- Works well in combination with vacuum truck operations.

## Strengths
- Significantly reduces manual exposure to high-pressure jets.
- High productivity for tube bundle work.
- Good safety record when properly used and maintained.

## Limitations / Notes
- Higher capital cost than basic manual systems.
- Best suited for repetitive, high-volume tube bundle work rather than general surface cleaning.
- Requires good maintenance and trained operators.

## Relevant Never Rules
- Never operate automated Peinemann-style systems without proper training and competency verification.
- Never bypass entanglement prevention or feed control safety features.
