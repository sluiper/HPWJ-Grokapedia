# Uraca (Germany) - Manufacturer Reference

**Category**: High-Pressure Pump OEM

## Overview
Uraca is a long-established German manufacturer of high-pressure pumps and systems with a solid reputation in industrial cleaning applications.

## Key Products
- High-pressure plunger pumps
- Complete water jetting units
- Industrial cleaning systems

## Notable Safety & Design Features
- Traditional robust German pump design.
- Good reliability record.
- Suitable for a wide range of industrial cleaning tasks.

## Specific Guidance Relevant to Anabeeb
- Solid, reliable option for general high-pressure water jetting work.
- Less specialized in ultra-high pressure or advanced automation compared to some competitors.
- Good European alternative when seeking proven pump technology.

## Strengths
- Long history and established reputation.
- Reliable performance.
- Good European engineering standards.

## Limitations / Notes
- Less prominent in the most advanced robotic/automated segments compared to Hammelmann or Peinemann.
- Not as widely referenced in some global HPWJ safety literature as NLB, WOMA, or StoneAge.
