# NLB Corp (USA) - Manufacturer Reference

**Category**: Major High-Pressure Pump OEM (Triplex)

## Overview
NLB is one of the leading manufacturers of diesel-powered high-pressure water jetting pumps, widely used in oil & gas and industrial cleaning applications. They have strong field support, particularly in the Middle East and North America.

## Key Products
- Diesel triplex plunger pumps (various horsepower ratings)
- Complete water jetting units (pump + engine + controls)
- Associated tooling and accessories

## Notable Safety & Design Features
- Strong emphasis on pre-operational checks (fluid levels, rupture disc condition, area clearance).
- Clear rule: **Do not leave the unit running unattended**.
- Good documentation on rupture disc inspection and replacement.
- Robust design suited for harsh field conditions.

## Specific Guidance Relevant to Anabeeb
- Pre-use inspection should include engine oil, pump oil, coolant, fuel, and DEF (where fitted).
- Area/other-end-of-unit checks are important before starting.
- Annual third-party inspections are expected.
- Strong focus on hose condition and whip check installation.

## Strengths
- Reliable field performance
- Good parts and service support in MEA region
- Clear safety messaging in manuals

## Limitations / Notes
- More focused on traditional triplex pump units than advanced robotic/automated systems (compared to Hammelmann or Peinemann).

## Official Documentation & Resources
- NLB official website: https://www.nlbcorp.com
- Check NLB operation and maintenance manuals for the specific model in use.

## Relevant Never Rules
- Never leave the pump unit running unattended.
- Never bypass or ignore rupture disc condition checks.
