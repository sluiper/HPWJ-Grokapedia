# Safetech Protection – Manufacturer Summary (UHP PPE)

**Source:** Safetech New Catalogue (EN) UHP 04-2022  
**Status:** Verified from manufacturer catalogue. For PPE selection only; always cross-check current certification and test data with supplier before purchase.

---

## 1. Positioning

Safetech Protection specialises in protective clothing engineered specifically for high-pressure and ultra-high-pressure waterjetting / waterblasting. Over 30 years waterjetting market experience. Focus: maximum jet resistance + ergonomic comfort + thermal management for long-duration work (critical in KSA heat).

Brand: SAFETECH® / AQUA SHIELD®. Distributed globally; relevant for Anabeeb UHP (40k) and high-pressure manual work.

| Model | Max Test Pressure | Nozzle / Flow Conditions | Linear Speed | Result |
|-------|-------------------|--------------------------|--------------|--------|

## 3. Key Product Lines

### UAP Light (Ultra Light)
- Intended for surface preparation / shipyard work.
- Jacket, Trousers, Overalls (sizes XS–XL).
- Weight example: ~2.6 kg overall.
- Striped protected areas clearly marked.

### UAP Max (Ultra Flex)
- Intended for service companies and general high-pressure / UHP work.
- Jacket, Trousers, Overalls, Gloves (one-size), Gaiters (one-size).
- Weight example: ~4.7 kg overall.
- Higher protection envelope (up to 45k psi class).

### Convertible Jacket / Vest → Apron
- Extension piece (HJ-EX-UF-UN) zips on to convert jacket or vest into apron for partial-protection tasks.

### Accessories
- Neck Protection (one-size).
- Hood (one-size, removable).
- High-resistance hygienic underwear (reduces friction).
- Hose protective covers (1 m / 3 m / 5 m) – absorb rupture energy / muffle jet and whip. **Does not replace whip-checks.**
- Whipsocks (multiple models for common hose sizes).

### Air Comfort System (Thermal Management – KSA Critical)
- Active cooling: reduces temperature **inside the outfit to 18 °C**.
- Capacity for **up to 3 simultaneous hydrojet operators**.
- Integrated air purification.
- 3 × 12 m thermally insulated hoses + trolley.
- Power: 220 V single-phase; min air 60 pcm @ 5.0 bar (75 psi).
- Weight ~65 kg.
- Code: HJ-AC-220V.

Highly relevant for Anabeeb Eastern Province summer work in full 20/30 or Turtle/Safetech suits (links to MCR-033 heat-stress controls).

### Impermeable Waterproof Overalls
- Separate product line for wet blasting / airless painting / chemical splash. **Not** rated for high-pressure jet penetration. Can be used with Air Comfort.

## 4. Technology Notes

- “Ultra flex” fabric developed specifically for hydrojetting.
- Withstands high-pressure jets without breakthrough to opposite side (under test conditions).
- Impermeable outer cover reduces water-mist accumulation (less weight + discomfort).
- Ergonomic cut for full range of jetting movements.
- Removable neck protector / hood to preserve field of vision.
- High-quality zippers, side/back adjustments on jacket/vest.

## 5. Selection Guidance for Anabeeb

| Job Pressure / Type | Recommended Safetech Path | Notes |
|---------------------|---------------------------|-------|
| Manual ≤25k psi | UAP Light or equivalent TST 10/28–20/30 | Confirm exact test match |
| Manual / UHP 25–40k psi | UAP Max or Turtle Suit (per OPS-P-019) | Turtle Suit still named in Anabeeb procedure for 40k |
| Long-duration summer work | Any rated suit + **Air Comfort** system | Directly supports MCR-033 |
| Hose connection protection | Hose cover + whipsock + whip-check | Layered defence |
| Partial body exposure | Convertible apron configuration | |

Always verify current CE / local certification, size fit, and that protected (striped) zones cover the expected line-of-fire for the specific task. PPE remains last line of defence after hierarchy of methods (MCR-039).

---

## 6. Gaps / Cautions

- Catalogue test data are laboratory conditions; real-world multi-orifice, different stand-off, or damaged fabric can reduce performance.
- No quantitative comfort or durability hours published in this catalogue.
- Compatibility with other brands (TST, TurtleSkin) not stated – do not mix certified systems without manufacturer confirmation.
- Air Comfort requires clean compressed air supply and power at the work face.
