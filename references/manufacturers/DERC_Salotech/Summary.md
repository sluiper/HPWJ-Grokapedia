# DERC Salotech (Netherlands) - Manufacturer Reference

**Category**: High-Pressure Water Jetting Equipment & Accessories Supplier

## Overview
DERC Salotech is a Dutch company specializing in high-pressure water jetting equipment, pumps, tooling, and safety accessories. They supply a wide range of products for industrial cleaning, including rated whip checks / hose restraints (including Red Dog style products).

## Key Products
- High-pressure pumps and units
- Tooling and lances
- Rated whip checks and hose restraints (including Red Dog line)
- Safety equipment and accessories

## Notable Safety & Design Features
- Supplies properly rated whip checks suitable for high-pressure water jetting service.
- Focus on complete system solutions for industrial cleaning.
- Products aligned with European safety standards.

## Specific Guidance Relevant to Anabeeb
- DERC Salotech / Red Dog whip checks can be used in HPWJ when they have a suitable Working Load Limit (WLL) for the pressure and hose size.
- Always verify the rating of the specific whip check against the job requirements.
- Good European supplier option for rated safety accessories.

## Strengths
- Supplies rated whip checks suitable for HPWJ.
- Wide range of water jetting equipment and accessories.
- European manufacturing and standards.

## Limitations / Notes
