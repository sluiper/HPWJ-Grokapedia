# WOMA (Germany) - Manufacturer Reference

**Category**: Pump + Multi-Gun Systems

## Overview
WOMA is a major German manufacturer of high-pressure water jetting pumps and systems. They are particularly known for their safety-focused design principles and multi-gun capability.

## Key Products
- EcoMaster series high-pressure units
- Twin-Jet / multi-gun simultaneous operation systems (up to 6 guns)
- ATEX-certified pumps for hazardous areas (Zone 2)
- Wireless remote control options
- Safety foot valves and accessories

## Notable Safety & Design Features
- **Pressureless-at-rest / fail-safe circulation design**: The system is pressureless when the high-pressure gun is not activated. This is a core safety principle.
- Strong ATEX options for classified hazardous areas.
- Good support for simultaneous multi-operator work.

## Specific Guidance Relevant to Anabeeb
- Verify pressureless-at-rest functionality during pre-use inspection on WOMA units.
- Multi-gun / twin-pump operations require specific coordination procedures (pressure changes by one operator affect others).
- Excellent choice when working in potentially explosive atmospheres or when multiple operators need to work from one pump.

## Strengths
- Pressureless-at-rest safety design
- Multi-gun capability
- Strong hazardous area (ATEX) options

## Limitations / Notes
- Multi-gun operations require additional team coordination and training.

## Relevant Never Rules
- Never operate WOMA units without confirming pressureless-at-rest functionality (where applicable).
- Never start multi-gun operations without agreed coordination procedures.
