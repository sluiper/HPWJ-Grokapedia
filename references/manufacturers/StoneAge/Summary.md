# StoneAge Tools (USA) - Manufacturer Reference

**Category**: Tooling & Automation Specialist (Not a Pump Manufacturer)

## Overview
StoneAge is a leading specialist in high-pressure water jetting **tooling**, not pumps. They design and manufacture rotating nozzles, flex lances, automated systems, and safety accessories. Their products are frequently paired with pumps from NLB and other OEMs.

## Key Products
- Rotating nozzles (Barracuda and similar)
- Flex lance systems
- Hand lances and shrouds
- Automated and semi-automated tooling
- Safety modules and accessories

## Notable Safety & Design Features
- Strong guidance on **wear life** (typically 20–60 hours depending on water quality and pressure).
- **Corrosion pitting** identified as a common precursor to tool failure.
- Reaction force guidance includes both the 250 N limit and the **1/3 body weight rule** plus barrel length geometry.
- Focus on reducing operator fatigue through rotating tooling.

## Specific Guidance Relevant to Anabeeb
- Always inspect nozzles for wear and corrosion pitting before use.
- Follow scheduled replacement intervals rather than running to failure.
- Use proper barrel length and stance to manage reaction force.
- StoneAge tooling works best when matched with a suitable pump from a reputable OEM.

## Strengths
- Excellent rotating and flex lance tooling
- Good safety and wear life documentation
- Practical field-focused guidance

## Limitations / Notes
- **Does not manufacture pumps** — always paired with pumps from NLB, WOMA, or similar.
- Best used as part of a complete system rather than standalone.

## Relevant Never Rules
- Never use worn or corroded nozzles beyond recommended life.
